"DISTRIBUTED SERVER"

Distributed cloud is the application of cloud computing technologies to interconnect data and applications served from multiple geographic locations.
Distributed, in an information technology (IT) context,
,means that something is shared among multiple systems which may also be in different locations.
Distributed cloud speeds communications for global services and enables more responsive communications for specific regions.
Some examples of this are Folding@home, BOINC and SETI@home.

In a volunteer cloud, the resources of member computers are connected through a single service or hub to collaboratively construct and configure cloud infrastructure.
