"APPLICATION OF CLOUD COMPUTING"





1. Scalable Usage:
This means that you will only need to pay for the computing resources you use. This helps in managing spikes in demands without the need to permanently invest in computer hardware.
Eg. Netflix, for instance, leverages this potential of cloud computing to its advantage.


2. Chatbots:
The expanded computing power and capacity of the cloud enables us to store information about user preferences.
Eg. Siri, Alexa and Google Assistant - all are cloud-based natural-language intelligent bots.


3. Communication:
The cloud allows users to enjoy network-based access to communication tools like emails and calendars.
Eg. Most of the messaging and calling apps like Skype and WhatsApp are also based on cloud infrastructure.

4. Productivity:
Office tools like Microsoft Office 365 and Google Docs use cloud computing, allowing you to use your most-productive tools over the internet.


5. Backup and recovery:
When you choose cloud for data storage the responsibility of your information also lies with your service provider. This saves you from the capital outlay for building infrastructure and maintenance.
Eg. Dropbox, Google Drive and Amazon S3 are popular examples of cloud backup solutions.

6. Big data analytics:
Cloud computing enables data scientists to tap into any organizational data to analyze it for patterns and insights, find correlations make predictions, forecast future crisis and help in data backed decision making.
Eg. There are many open source big data tools that are based on the cloud for instance Hadoop, Cassandra, HPCC etc.


7. Social Networking:
Social Media is the most popular and often overlooked application of cloud computing.
Eg. Facebook, LinkedIn, MySpace, Twitter, and many other social networking sites use cloud computing.
