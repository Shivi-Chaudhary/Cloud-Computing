"CLOUD COMPUTING"

Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services—servers, storage, databases, networking, software, analytics and more—over the Internet (“the cloud”).
Companies offering these computing services are called cloud providers and typically charge for cloud computing services based on usage, similar to how you are billed for water or electricity at home.
Eg: Yahoo!, GMail, Hotmail-

Cloud computing relies on sharing of resources to achieve coherence and economies of scale, similar to a public utility.
cloud computing allows enterprises to get their applications up and running faster,
with improved manageability and less maintenance,
and that it enables IT teams to more rapidly adjust resources to meet fluctuating and unpredictable demand.
